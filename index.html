About EducateHacks

Educatehacks is a hackathon run by students for students. Brought to you by Amogh Ganjikunta, Keevan Leech & Aaryan Chitnis. The Primary theme for this hackathon will be edTech. More details will be released closer to the start date for this event. Sign up now to reserve your place!

FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for Students and Professionals from all over the world! EducateHacks is the right place for anyone who's interested in learning, problem solving, innovating and collaborating!

01

How much will it cost?

Registration and participation for EducateHacks is completely free, thanks to support from our sponsors

02

How can I register?

The registration process for this hackathon is done through devpost.

Click here to register!

03

What if I don't know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level.

04

What are the prizes?

We are going to announce some really exciting prizes and incentives/guest presenters soon. Stay tuned for further updates!

05

Can we apply as a team?

Yes! We believe that you’re stronger as a team than apart. You can form teams of 2-6 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and development skills.
